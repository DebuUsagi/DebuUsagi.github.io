Description of Main Game. Press the button to go straight to their page.

Lab : Place to Level-up Nox, Reobtain Nox, Sell, and etc.
Party : Menu for Leviathan and Party configuration.
Quest : The area to go through a variety of Quests to progress the game.
Raid : Defeat Raids with the help of Alliance.
Alliance : To gather Allies to help defeat Raids.
Warehouse : To shop, hold items, and exchange currency.
Mailbox : Raid reward and messages of Alliance is found here.
O.D : To spend O.D to buy early game items, such as Nyang, Gold Scarabs, Potato(AP Recovery), and Super Rare nox, such as Raid drops.

World Map : To change Areas to do Area specific Quests.
Menu : To adjust game settings.
