- At the bottom right of the Main Game screen is Alliance[동맹]. The number displayed is current number and the max number of
- Alliance members a Player can have. The max number of Alliance members is 50.

- Pressing the button, Players will be able to enter a Player's name. If the Player does not enter then it will bring up random Players.
- Once an invitation is recieved, it will show in the [신청 알림] tab.

- Choose [수락하기] to accept and [거걸하기] to deny an invitation.

- Press Alliance List[동맹 목록] to display your Alliance list.
- Players in the Alliance List will be able to fight Raids and get rewards from defeating your Raids and vice-versa.
